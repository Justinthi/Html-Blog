Edmonton at a Glance

Edmonton is a vibrant city that combines natural scenery with urban attractions, making it a great destination for short trips or weekend travel. The river valley offers extensive trails and viewpoints that feel surprisingly close to downtown. The city also has lively neighbourhoods with local restaurants, cafés, and shops that are easy to explore on foot.

What Visitors Enjoy

  • West Edmonton Mall: The largest shopping and entertainment complex in North America, featuring waterparks, theme parks, and hundreds of stores
  • Royal Alberta Museum: Explore natural and human history in Western Canada's largest museum
  • Muttart Conservatory: Unique pyramid-shaped glass structures housing diverse plant life
  • Fort Edmonton Park: Canada's largest living history museum, located in the River Valley

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Edmonton depends on your interests: Visit in the Summer (June-August) for the festivals and outdoor activities. Fall (September-October) provides beautiful foliage with fewer crowds and lower costs. Winter (December-February) is ideal for snow sports and unique events like Ice on Whyte but is very cold. Finally Spring (March-May) brings blooming flowers and mild weather, great for a quieter trip before the summer rush.

One-Day Itinerary

  1. Morning: Start your day at West Edmonton Mall the largest mall Edmonton has to offer. Containing 800+ stores, you can grab breakfast and explore unique attractions like the World Waterpark with its indoor pool or the Galaxyland amusement park with roller coasters.
  2. Late Morning: Visit the Royal Alberta Museum to learn about Alberta's natural and human history
  3. Afternoon At this time, visit the Muttart Conservatory, known for its glass pyramids that house botanical collections from around the world. A unique architectural and natural spot perfect for an hour or so of exploration. 
  4. Evening: End your day by enjoying dinner at one of the many restaurants along Whyte Avenue or Jasper Avenue downtown. After your meal, feel free to experience the nightlife that Edmonton has to offer by visiting a pub or catching a live performance at one of the many local venues.
